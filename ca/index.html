About Radiance

Radiance aims to provide a platform for budding researchers, innovators, and technocrats so that they can experiment with their knowledge and explore the fields of engineering.

About CA Program

We at Radiance aspire to reach out to college students across the globe to spark their interest in Research and Engineering fields and provide them with a platform to pursue the same, and the CA Program helps us realize this vision. This is an excellent opportunity for all of us to work together and make this edition of Radiance a grand success.
